Welcome to Diamond Tech

We are an ICT company incorporated in Zimbabwe and South Africa with a key focus on hardware, software, consumables and electricals. Check out our website to learn about us and reach out to us if you have any questions.


About Us

Our business model is to partner with global ICT brands since 2018. Founded by Fortune Makoni, we are a proudly youth-owned company.

Our Products

We are suppliers of the biggest PC and Laptop brands such as Apple, HP, Lenovo, etc. We also supply PC and Laptop spare and parts such as screens and accessories.

Printers and Cartridge

We are suppliers and commercial and domestic printers such as Canon, HP and Fijitsu. Our cartridges also come with a 180-day money back guarantee.

Cellphones and Tablets
